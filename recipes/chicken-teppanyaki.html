Chicken Teppanyaki


Description

Inspired by B cafe in Quincy, MA, this dish makes for a relatively inexpensive and somewhat simple option to experience the Asian cuisine.


Ingredients

  • Sesame teriyaki wok sauce
  • Boneless skinless chicken
  • Rice
  • Boolean cube
  • Corn starch
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt
  • Lemon juice

Recipe

  1. 1 cup of rice is good for 2 people
  2. Rice:water - 1:2 ratio
  3. 1 Boolean cube in rice
  4. Lid on
  5. Medium high to boil
  6. Then almost all the way low for 15-20mins (no moisture - no slide)
  7. Then pull off the heat and let sit for another little bit

  8. Separate chicken thighs
  9. Pat chicken dry
  10. Cut the fat off
  11. Cut chicken into strips
  12. Spread out chicken
  13. Layer of salt, onion powder, garlic
  14. Good amount of corn starch (everything should be coated)
  15. Olive oil on pan - put high heat right away
  16. Wait til pan hot (want chicken to sizzle immediately)
  17. Place chicken on pan (should sizzle)
  18. Put heat down to medium high
  19. Wait for crispy look to flip them all (golden brown color) - take them all out
  20. Remove most of the oil
  21. Turn heat down to low
  22. Quarter bottle of teriyaki sauce
  23. Cap full of lemon juice
  24. Mix
  25. Put in all the chicken
  26. Toss it around in the pan

  27. Butter, salt, and fluff the rice
  28. Table spoon or two of butter
  29. Teaspoon of salt
  30. Fluff
