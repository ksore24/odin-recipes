Muffins

Description

Mom's homemade muffins. Basic ingredients with chocolate chips. Nothing better.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Vanilla
  • Chocolate Chips

Recipe

Makes 14 Muffins
  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Mix 3.5 cups of flour, 1.25 cups sugar, 1 tbspn baking powder, 1.5 tspn salt, 12 tbspns butter, 2 eggs, 1 cup milk, 1 tsp vanilla, 2 cups chocolate chips
  3. Bake for 15 minutes
  4. Check for optimal cook-time with fork
